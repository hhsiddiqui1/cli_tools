## Data Sources

**Where to find this data:**

**Merchant data:** Your database - `auth_users` table
- ABNs, payment methods, bank details, PayIDs

**Transaction data:** Your database - `trans_transactions` table OR Fiserv reports
- Transaction volumes, amounts, dates

**Payout data:** Your database - `trans_payout` table
- Payout amounts, methods, statuses

**Cash withdrawal data:** ANZ bank statements
- All cash withdrawals from your ANZ account

**System data:** Your backend admin endpoints
- `/admin/search_users` - Merchant list
- `/transactions/search_transactions_by_user` - Transaction data
- `/payout/search_user_payouts` - Payout data
- `/admin/get_all_auditlogs` - Audit information
